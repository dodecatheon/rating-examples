Examples benham1.csv, benham2.csv, and benham3.csv are used to test for Chicken
Dilemma criterian compliance.  They were taken from

http://lists.electorama.com/pipermail/election-methods-electorama.com/2016-October/000599.html

For CW compliance, the winners should be A, C and A respectively. If CW is failed,
then the burier candidate B will win all three examples.
